Games consoles

Here are some less known facts about games consoles

  • The Nintendo entertainment system was not the first games console. the Magnavox Odyssey came first being released in 1972.
  • Nintendo asked Sony for help to develop a disk sytem for the SNES, then turned their back on them giving rise to the playstation.
  • Sega similarly asked microsoft for help with the dreamcast, after the engineers threw a couple of laptops together and made a pitch that gave birth to the Xbox.
